# UpsurgeIQ V2 Roadmap: Website Builder for Small Businesses

## Vision

Expand UpsurgeIQ from an AI-powered PR & marketing platform into a **full-service communications agency** by offering simple, affordable website building and hosting services for small businesses.

## Core Concept

> "Simple and cheap website with a selection of add-on tools for small businesses. We build and host the website and set up domains, etc."

This positions UpsurgeIQ as a one-stop shop for small business communications needs:
- **Current (V1)**: PR, press releases, social media, campaigns, journalist outreach
- **Future (V2)**: Website creation, hosting, domain management, and integrated marketing tools

---

## Key Features

### 1. Website Builder
- **Drag-and-drop interface** for easy website creation
- **Pre-built templates** for common business types (restaurants, retail, services, etc.)
- **AI-powered content generation** for website copy (leveraging existing AI capabilities)
- **Mobile-responsive designs** out of the box
- **SEO optimization** built-in

### 2. Hosting & Infrastructure
- **Managed hosting** - fully handled by UpsurgeIQ
- **SSL certificates** included automatically
- **CDN integration** for fast global delivery
- **Automatic backups** and version control
- **99.9% uptime guarantee**

### 3. Domain Management
- **Domain registration** directly through the platform
- **Domain transfer** support for existing domains
- **DNS management** interface
- **Email forwarding** setup
- **Subdomain management**

### 4. Integrated Marketing Tools
- **Blog integration** with AI-assisted writing
- **Contact forms** with lead capture
- **Newsletter signup** integration
- **Social media feed** embedding
- **Analytics dashboard** for website traffic
- **SEO tools** for optimization

### 5. Add-On Services
- **E-commerce** functionality (product catalog, shopping cart, payment processing)
- **Booking systems** for appointments and reservations
- **Live chat** integration
- **Custom forms** and workflows
- **Member portals** for restricted content
- **Multi-language support**

## Integration with Existing Platform

### Synergies
1. **Unified dashboard** - manage website, PR, and social media from one place
2. **AI content generation** - use the same AI for website copy, press releases, and social posts
3. **Cross-promotion** - automatically publish press releases to website blog
4. **Consistent branding** - use business dossier to maintain brand voice across all channels
5. **Analytics consolidation** - unified reporting for website, social media, and campaigns

### Technical Architecture
- **Separate subdomain** for website builder (e.g., `sites.upsurgeiq.com`)
- **Shared authentication** - single sign-on across all services
- **API-first design** - allow third-party integrations
- **Modular codebase** - website builder as separate microservice

## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Market Saturation
- **Mitigation**: Differentiate through AI-powered content and integrated PR/marketing tools

### Risk 2: Technical Complexity
- **Mitigation**: Start with MVP, iterate based on feedback, use proven technologies

### Risk 3: Support Burden
- **Mitigation**: Invest in comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and chatbot support

### Risk 4: Hosting Costs
- **Mitigation**: Tiered pricing based on usage, partner with reliable hosting providers for volume discounts
