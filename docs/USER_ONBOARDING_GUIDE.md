## Welcome to UpsurgeIQ

UpsurgeIQ is your AI-powered PR and marketing platform that helps you create professional press releases, manage media contacts, and run intelligent campaigns.

## Getting Started (First 15 Minutes)

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit: https://upsurgeiq.com/signup
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a secure password
4. Click "Create Account"
5. Check your email for welcome message

**What happens next:**
- You'll be redirected to the dashboard
- A welcome email will arrive within 2 minutes
- You'll start with a 14-day free trial

---

### Step 2: Complete Your Business Profile

**Why this matters:** Your business profile helps our AI generate better, more personalized content.

1. Click "Settings" in the sidebar
2. Navigate to "Business Profile"
3. Fill in:
   - Company name
   - Industry (select from dropdown)
   - Company description
   - Target audience
   - Brand voice (Professional, Friendly, or Inspirational)
   - Key products/services

**Time required:** 5-10 minutes

**Pro tip:** The more detailed your profile, the better your AI-generated content will be!

---

### Step 3: Create Your First Press Release

1. Click "Press Releases" in sidebar
2. Click "+ New Press Release" button
3. Choose creation method:
   - **AI-Assisted** (Recommended for first-timers)
   - **Manual** (Write from scratch)

**Using AI-Assisted:**

1. Enter your press release topic (e.g., "New product launch")
2. Provide key details:
   - What is the announcement?
   - Why is it newsworthy?
   - Who is the target audience?
3. Click "Generate Draft"
4. Review and edit the AI-generated content
5. Click "Save as Draft"

**Time required:** 10-15 minutes

### Social Media Integration

**Connect Your Accounts:**

### Media Lists

**Access Pre-Built Lists:**

1. Click "Media Lists" in sidebar
2. Browse by:
   - Industry (Tech, Finance, Healthcare, etc.)
   - Region (UK, US, Europe, etc.)
   - Beat (Business, Lifestyle, Sports, etc.)

**Create Custom Lists:**

1. Click "+ New Media List"
2. Name your list
3. Add contacts manually or import CSV
4. Save list

---

### AI Assistant (Pro & Scale Plans)

**Start a Conversation:**

1. Click "AI Assistant" in sidebar
2. Type your question or request

**Example questions:**
- "How do I write a compelling headline?"
- "What's the best time to send a press release?"
- "Help me craft a social media strategy"

**Voice Feature (Pro & Scale):**
- Click microphone icon
- Speak your question
- Get voice or text response
