## Premium Add-On Services

### Intelligent Campaign Lab (¬£299/month - Standalone)

The Intelligent Campaign Lab is available as a standalone service for any subscription tier (Starter, Pro, or Scale).

**Features**
- AI-powered advertising campaign optimization
- Multi-variant creative testing (4-6 different ad variations per testing round)
- Testing across different psychological angles and value propositions
- Real-time performance monitoring and feedback collection
- Automatic identification of winning variations
- Continuous redeployment and testing of new variations
- Campaign performance analytics and detailed reporting
- Conversational AI assistant for campaign strategy and management
- Support for campaigns on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and X

**Important Notes**
- Ad spend is managed and paid directly by the user to the respective ad platform (Facebook Business Manager, etc.)
- upsurgeIQ does not handle or charge for ad spend
- No performance guarantees or return on investment (ROI) guarantees
- Results depend on market conditions, audience targeting, and other external factors

### Additional Media Lists (¬£4 per list per release)

- Available to all subscription tiers
- Add specialized media lists beyond the default and included lists
- Lists are curated by industry, region, publication type, and topic
- One-time purchase per press release distribution

## Onboarding and Setup Services

### Initial Dossier Building

All subscription tiers include comprehensive onboarding that includes:

- **Business Research:** Manus AI automatically researches the client's website, industry, and competitive landscape
- **Brand Voice Definition:** Client selects tone (Formal, Friendly, Inspirational, Witty, Educational) and style (Concise, Detailed, Story-Driven, Data-Driven)
- **Industry Classification:** SIC code selection for precise industry categorization
- **AI Image Settings:** Client defines preferred image style, mood, and color palette
- **Social Media Account Connection:** OAuth-based secure connection to Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and X
- **Sports Team Integration (Scale Plan):** For motorsport and professional sports clients, series/league selection and automatic schedule ingestion

### Ongoing Dossier Management

- Monthly updates to journalist and editor media lists based on industry and topic
- Continuous refinement of brand voice and messaging based on usage
- LinkedIn profile monitoring for new hire content generation

## Data and Privacy Services

### Media List Management

- **Default Lists:** Pre-curated journalist and editor lists by industry, region, and publication type
- **User-Uploaded Lists:** Ability to upload custom journalist lists in CSV format
- **List Verification:** Manus AI validates and verifies each contact through public web searches
- **Share & Earn Program:** Users can opt-in to contribute verified contacts to the master upsurgeIQ database in exchange for subscription credits (¬£0.10 per verified contact)
- **GDPR Compliance:** One-time GDPR-compliant introductory email sent to new contacts with clear opt-out option

### Data Protection

- All personal data processed in accordance with UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018
- Data Processing Addendum provided separately
- Privacy Policy provided separately
- Secure data storage and encryption
- Regular security audits and compliance checks
